What is Dragon Ball Z?


Dragon Ball Z is an adapted, anime category, tv serieres from the final 324 chapters of the manga series; which were published in Weekly Shonen Jump comic from 1988 to 1995. It premiered in Japan on Fuji Television on April 26, 1989, taking over its predecessor's time slot, and ran for 291 episodes until its conclusion on January 31, 1996.
